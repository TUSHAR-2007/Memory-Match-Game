Flip & Match

Tap any card to flip it. Then tap a second card. If both cards show the same icon — it's a match!

✅ Matched pairs disappear from the board
❌ Mismatches flip back face-down
⏱️ Clear the board before the timer runs out
⚡

Score & IQ

Chain matches in a row to build COMBO streaks and earn bonus points!

🔥 +10 pts per match · Combo bonus grows fast
🧠 IQ rises on matches, drops on misses
💡 Use up to 3 Hints to reveal all cards briefly
